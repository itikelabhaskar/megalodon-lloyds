Here are some details about what you will be doing at the hackathon. We can't wait to see what you create!

## Devices & Set-Up

- Please bring your own personal devices to participate.
- You will code in a sandbox environment enabled for these 2 days. Login details will be provided at the venue.

## The Challenges

Your team will be allocated a challenge based on a problem statement. The theme for the Hackathon is Agentic AI.

## Pitches

On day two, all teams will pitch their game to their assigned coaches. Coaches will then shortlist teams to present to our judging panel.

Teams will have 5 minutes each to pitch to coaches. Shortlisted hackers will have 8 minutes + 2 minutes for Q&A when pitching to judges.

You can use slides, a live demo, or any other format you like to present your game. Be creative and have fun!

- Pitches to the coaches will begin at 12:15pm
- Pitches to the judges will begin at 2:15pm

## Judging Criteria

Our judges will be considering the following when casting their vote:

Value and Proposition (20%)

**Business Value:**Â Does the solution address a real problem or opportunity? Is it likely to deliver measurable impact for users, customers, or the organisation?

**Market Overview:**Â Is there a clear understanding of the market landscape? Has the team identified competitors, gaps, and potential for adoption or growth?

Agentic Feasibility (20%)

**Agentic Architecture:**Â Does the solution leverage agentic AI principles (autonomy, decision-making, multi-agent collaboration)? Is the approach technically sound and feasible within current or near-future capabilities?

**Scalability & Integration:**Â Can the solution be realistically implemented and scaled within your organisation's tech stack? Are integration points with existing systems considered?

Innovation & Originality (20%)

**Creativity:**Â Is the idea novel or does it offer a fresh approach to an existing challenge? Does it stand out from other submissions?

**Theme Alignment:**Â How well does the solution embody the hackathon's theme of Agentic AI?

Technical Execution (20%)

**Functionality:**Â Is there a working prototype or MVP? Does it demonstrate the core features and agentic capabilities?

**Quality & Robustness:**Â Is the solution stable, well-documented, and free from major bugs? Is the technical approach well-justified?

Usability & Accessibility (10%)

**User Experience:**Â Is the solution intuitive and easy to use? Can users quickly understand and interact with the agentic AI features?

# Code of Conduct

We aim to provide a friendly and supportive environment for all attendees. Let's ensure everyone gets the most from the day.

## Please follow the Lloyds Banking Group values

## Inclusive

Recognise and celebrate the things that differentiate us, be inclusive in your actions and language. Help us make the event something for everyone to benefit from.

## Sustainability

Travel sustainably and minimise waste at the event. Don't throw away reusable giveaway items - if you don't need/want it, don't accept it.

## Trust

Assume peers are working in our best interests and trust them in their endeavours.

## Bold

Be prepared to meet new people, learn new things and experiment with technology. Push yourself out of your comfort zone, do something new and enjoy the experience.

## People First

Treat each other with respect and go the extra mile to support your peers. Be prepared to provide those around you with help, guidance and support.

If you witness or experience inappropriate behaviour, please address it respectfully or speak to one of the event organisers.
